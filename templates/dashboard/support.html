Frequently Asked Questions

To make a deposit, go to the Deposit page from your dashboard. Choose your preferred payment method (bank transfer, credit card, or cryptocurrency), enter the amount, and follow the instructions. Deposits are usually processed instantly.

Withdrawal processing times vary by method: Bank transfers take 1-3 business days, cryptocurrency withdrawals take 10-60 minutes, and PayPal withdrawals take 1-2 business days.

Investment minimums vary by plan: Cryptocurrency ($500), Stock Trading ($1,000), Forex Trading ($750), and Bonds & Fixed Income ($2,000).

Go to your account settings, find the Security section, and click "Enable 2FA". You'll need to download an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy, then scan the QR code provided.

Investment cancellations depend on the specific plan and timing. Generally, you can cancel within 24 hours of investment without penalty. After that, early withdrawal fees may apply.
